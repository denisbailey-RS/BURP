BURP Examples
-------------

Example Query:
"Why do birds migrate?"

Filled Schema:

Entities:
  - Birds
  - Seasonal environments
  - Food availability

Relations:
  - Birds depend on seasonal environments.
  - Seasonal changes affect food availability.

Operator:
  - Explain

Constraints:
  - Preserve ecological causality.
  - Maintain directionality of relations.

Recursive Structure:
  1. Identify the biological subject (birds).
  2. Identify environmental factors (seasons, food).
  3. Connect environmental changes to bird behavior.
  4. Check that explanation preserves causal direction.

Output:
  Birds migrate because seasonal changes reduce food availability, and migration
  allows them to maintain access to necessary resources.
