BURP Instructions (How to Use the Schema)
-----------------------------------------

Bailey’s Universal Reasoning Protocol (BURP) is a universal reasoning structure
that forces AI systems to think in a stable, transparent, drift-free format.

These instructions are intentionally simple and flexible. The AI uses its own
linguistic and conceptual abilities to fill the schema clearly.

How to Use BURP:

1. Take any user query.
2. Convert it into the RS-Reasoning schema.
3. Fill each section with content relevant to the query.
4. Ensure that all reasoning steps preserve the listed constraints.
5. Produce the final answer in the "Output" section.
6. Return both the structured reasoning and the final output.

Guiding Principles:

- The schema is fixed.
- The instructions are flexible.
- The AI uses its full language capacity to focus the answer.
- Drift is eliminated by staying inside the RS structure.
- The reasoning must remain interpretable and stable.
